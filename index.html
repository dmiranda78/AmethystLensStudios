Inspired by Nature

My work captures the haunting beauty of moody, storm-driven landscapes and the raw presence of portraiture steeped in the tones of early photography. Blending nature's turmoil with human truth, each image echoes the atmosphere of a forgotten time, revealing what lies just beneath the surface.

Drawn to Storms

Capturing the raw emotion of storm-lit skies, windswept landscapes, and the quiet drama of nature's darker moods. Deanna Miranda Photography is drawn to contrast, mystery, and moments that most overlook. Whether it's the quiet before the storm or the electric chaos of a thunderstorm, each image preserves the poetry of the weather.

Moods

Moments suspended between shadow and light, where landscapes become stories and ordinary places take on a cinematic presence.

I seek not perfection, but presence: those quiet, contemplative instants where light barely clings to the face and emotion drifts like clouds over a landscape. This is portraiture that speaks in a silence that lingers, that remembers.

Scenery

Storms rolling in. Empty roads. Quiet mornings. Light breaking through clouds. Scenery that feels less like a place, and more like a memory.
